Who I am

About Me

I am a Ph.D. Scholar at IIT Ropar, specializing in microarchitectural security and high-performance CPU design. My research bridges CPU performance optimization with secure architecture design.

I have extensive experience developing and validating architectural simulators for performance and energy analysis, and I am proficient in C/C++ programming with strong expertise in system-level modeling and hardware–software co-design.

My work has been published in leading venues including IEEE HOST and DATE, addressing critical challenges at the intersection of memory systems, cache architectures, and hardware security.
